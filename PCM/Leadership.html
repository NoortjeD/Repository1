Leadership Skills Workshop

Self Assignment

Obviously, I chose this workshop to work on one of my goals: to improve my leadership skills. Thanks to this workshop I have more insight in what it takes to be a leader, and what kind of leader I am.

If I'd have to pick which leadership style fits the most with me personally, I would say the coaching style does. I delegate, but am also still involved. Letting go would be a challenge for me.

During the workshop, we filled in a questionnaire which shows us what kind of leader we are. my results are shown in the graph on the right. According to this scheme, my greatest strength is 'divisor'. “You can't get there without others”. I really do believe that. A weakness of mine is that I tend to think too realistic, which limits me in letting my creativity go.
I would need someone who is more like a dreamer/doer, to balance out my strengths with theirs, and make the perfect team.



WHY(What do I stand for? Who am I as a leader? Where do I see myself as a future leader?)
Kindness, and honesty are very important values to me, which I want to portray as a leader. I also hope to be motivating.

HOW(How do I get there? What is my process?)
I think I will get there by staying true to who I am. I am a kind and honest person, but sometimes other factors get the best of me. I also think I should keep challenging myself in taking on projects that test my leadership, and always asking feedback. These will teach me more about myself, and what I should and shouldn’t do as a leader.

WHAT(what is the result of my leadership style?)
The result is that, together with my peers/team/colleagues, we will make a great product, while having fun and while learning from each other. At least, that’s what I hope to achieve as a leader.
