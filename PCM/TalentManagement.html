Talent Management Workshop

My vision on talent

I think everyone has something they´re good at. You just have to find it, and develop it into a talent. I also think you can create talent, even though you are not born with it.

My biggest inspiration

My role model is Hailee Steinfeld. She is a singer/actress from Los Angeles, and an overall kind person. What I admire most about her is that she always works really hard to achieve her goals and I aspire to do the same.

What I learned about myself from my VIA result

My top 5 are:

  1. Fairness
  2. Love
  3. Honesty
  4. Teamwork
  5. Kindness

My bottom 5 are:

  1. Self-regulation
  2. Gratitude
  3. Creativity
  4. Zest
  5. Spirituality

I am not surprised by these results. My values are to treat everyone the same, and to always be kind. I have noticed that that’s not always easy to be or do in practice, but I really try. I do think it is interesting that creativity is in my bottom 5, while I am here, making an assignment for a 'Creative Business' study...

Translation of my result into development of my talents

With these results, I can work on my leadership skills. I know what my strengths and weaknesses are.
