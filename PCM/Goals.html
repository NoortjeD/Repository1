Semester 4

1. To develop my leadership skills more.

General Reflection

This semester I didn't have a lot of leadership tasks. I was the producer for my Free Elective project, but that was about it. Though looking at the feedback I have gotten for that project, it looks like I did a good job (and it feels like it too). Besides that, I have taken some responsibility in some assignments. Responsibility is obviously an important characteristic for leadership.
So generally, with the leadership opportunities I had, I think I did well. I am not sure if I improved this skill though.

Semester 3

1. To develop my leadership skills more.

General Reflection

I don’t think I improved these skills. But considering the circumstances, I think I did alright. I’ve had multiple setbacks through the semester, which all lead me to be behind on work, tired, and very impatient. This showed in my leadership style. Looking at the feedback I got, it impacted some of my peers, but not all. It will not be easy to get back on track, but it’s already going better now than before Christmas Break.
