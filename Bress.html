Bodyshape Class at Bress

Promotional Video

2020

One of the writers for the HUB website (an online entertainment platform for BUas students) asked me and another student, Jessie van Balkom, to make a video in addition to her article. She wrote about a bodyshape class at Bress, a student Gym. Of course it is interesting to read about such a class, but seeing what that class is like, gives of course a way better impression!

Bress loved the video and asked us if they could use it as a promotion themselves. As you can see, they've uploaded the video to Facebook!

A Bodyshape class at BRESS! This is what it was all about. A lot of people doing sports together, having fun with Peter....
